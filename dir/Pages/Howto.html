1. Fill in Your Personal Details: Enter your Name (First Name and Last Name)
Provide your phone number
Enter your email address

2. Choose Delivery or Collection:

Select "Delivery" if you want your order delivered to your address.
Select "Collection" if you prefer to collect your order yourself (pickup).

3. Select Your Order Type:

A dropdown menu allows you to choose the type of order you want to place.

Options might include:
Shirts & Hoodies
Drinkware (Glasses, Mugs & Tumblers)
Other(Request a service and see if we can accomodate you)

4. Specify Your Order Details:

The details you need to provide will depend on the order type you selected.

For Shirts & Hoodies:
Choose the quantity for each item (shirts and hoodies) using the number input fields.
Specify the desired colors for each item in the text area provided.
Select the font option if you want text printed on your shirts/hoodies.
If chosen, enter the font name and size in the provided fields.
Choose the image option if you want an image printed.
Currently, there's no upload functionality, so refer to the instructions provided for emailing your image details.

For Drinkware (Glasses, Mugs & Tumblers):

Choose the quantity for each drinkware item (glasses, mugs, tumblers) using the number input fields.
Select the font option if you want text printed on your drinkware.
If chosen, enter the font name and size in the provided fields.

5. Review Your Order (Optional):

Although not explicitly mentioned, it's recommended to review your order details before submitting the form.
Double-check the quantities, colors, any customization options, and ensure your contact information is correct.

6. Submit Your Order:

Once you've filled out all the required details and reviewed your order (if desired), click the submit button to place your order.
The form should be programmed to send your order information for processing.
